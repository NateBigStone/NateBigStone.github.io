About Nathan

Nathan Gaut is a self-motivated, achievement-oriented professional with excellent communication and interpersonal skills. In addition to his service as both an employee and a consultant with the industry-leader in broadband services, he has professional experience in the design and implementation of commercial building systems and IT networks. More recently, Nathan has acquired hands-on experience and shown proficiency with web development tools not limited to: HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap, Ruby, JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, and has an aptitude for learning additional languages.
